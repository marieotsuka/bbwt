Type & Technology

Building Brands with Type, Week 3

July 24–28, 2023
School of Visual Arts

Overview

This week, we’ll review the relationship betwen type and technology, from the ways tools can help your type design workflow, to how typefaces can be activated through technology.

Design Goals

  • Complete your character set
  • Understand and apply effective spacing
  • Structure nodes and off-curves for refined designs
  • Begin explorations of your brand specimens

Technical Goals

  • Develop familiarity with tools to help your design process
  • Understand the nature of a digital font
  • Familiarize yourself with how variable font formats work
  • Learn how to setup stylistic alternates and other Open Type features
